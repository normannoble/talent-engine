## Assessment Context

M3 (Head of Engineering) is a senior leadership role requiring demonstrated ability to develop other managers, shape organizational capability, and contribute to company strategy. This assessment is typically conducted with input from executive leadership.

- Candidate prepares evidence packet documenting M3-level impact
- Sponsor presents case to assessment panel
- Panel evaluates evidence against M3 criteria
- Executive input is incorporated given the role's scope

### Rating Scale

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 1 - Not Yet | Operating at strong M2 level; organizational impact not yet demonstrated |
| 2 - Emerging | Shows M3 impact in some areas; needs broader demonstration or more time |
| 3 - Ready | Consistently demonstrates M3-level impact and capability |

*M3 readiness requires '3 - Ready' in most areas. This is a high bar for senior leadership.*
