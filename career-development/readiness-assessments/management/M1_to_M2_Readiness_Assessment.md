## How to Use This Assessment

This assessment evaluates readiness for Senior Engineering Manager (M2).
For each competency:

- Employee self-assesses with specific examples of M2-level work
- Manager assesses based on observed behavior and impact
- Discuss gaps and identify development actions

### Rating Scale

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 1 - Not Yet | Still operating at M1 level; requires significant development |
| 2 - Emerging | Shows M2 behaviors inconsistently; needs more practice and experience |
| 3 - Ready | Consistently demonstrates M2-level performance in this area |

*Promotion readiness requires '3 - Ready' in most areas, with no areas at '1 - Not Yet.'*
