## How to Use This Assessment

This assessment evaluates readiness for Senior Engineer (IC3). For each competency:

- **Employee self-assesses** with specific examples of IC3-level work
- **Manager assesses** based on observed behavior and impact
- **Discuss gaps** and identify development actions

## Rating Scale

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **1 - Not Yet** | Still operating at IC2 level; needs significant development |
| **2 - Emerging** | Shows IC3 behaviors inconsistently; needs more practice |
| **3 - Ready** | Consistently demonstrates IC3-level performance |

*Promotion readiness requires "3 - Ready" in most areas, with no areas at "1 - Not Yet."*
