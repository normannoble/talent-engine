## Assessment Context

E3 (CTO) is the senior-most technology leadership role—ultimate accountability for technology strategy, organization, and competitive advantage. This assessment evaluates readiness for that level of responsibility.

- Candidate prepares comprehensive evidence packet documenting CTO-level impact
- Sponsor (typically CEO) presents case to assessment panel
- Panel includes board member(s) and external perspective
- Assessment may include external validation (industry peers, references)

## Rating Scale

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 1 - Not Yet | Operating at strong E2 level; CTO-level impact not yet demonstrated |
| 2 - Emerging | Shows CTO impact in some areas; needs broader demonstration |
| 3 - Ready | Consistently demonstrates CTO-level impact and capability |

*CTO readiness requires '3 - Ready' in all core areas. This is the highest bar in the organization.*
