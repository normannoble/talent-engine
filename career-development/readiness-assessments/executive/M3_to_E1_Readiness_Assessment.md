## Assessment Context

E1 (Director) represents the transition from senior management to executive leadership—from contributing to strategy to owning domain outcomes. This assessment evaluates readiness for that transition.

- Candidate prepares evidence documenting domain-level impact and executive-level work
- Sponsor presents case to assessment panel
- Panel evaluates evidence against E1 criteria
- Executive input is incorporated given the role's scope

## Rating Scale

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 1 - Not Yet | Operating at strong M3 level; executive-level impact not yet demonstrated |
| 2 - Emerging | Shows E1 impact in some areas; needs broader demonstration or more time |
| 3 - Ready | Consistently demonstrates E1-level impact and capability |

*E1 readiness requires '3 - Ready' in most areas.*
