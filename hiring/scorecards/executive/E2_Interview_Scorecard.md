## Level Context: What E2 Looks Like

E2 is a **senior executive** who operates across multiple technology domains and partners closely with the CTO. Evaluate for:

- **Cross-domain leadership** — Ensures multiple domains work together and align with company strategy
- **Executive partnership** — Operates as a peer to other executives, not just a functional leader
- **Developing executives** — Grows E1s (Directors) toward Senior Director or CTO capability
- **External presence** — Represents the company's technology externally

**Differentiation from E1:** E1 owns a single domain; E2 operates across multiple domains with a general management perspective.
**Differentiation from E3 (CTO):** E2 partners with and deputizes for the CTO; E3 owns ultimate technology accountability.

**Note:** E2 is a senior executive hire. Look for demonstrated cross-domain impact, CTO partnership, and executive development experience.

**What to assess:** Leading across multiple domains, organizational effectiveness, strategic coherence.
