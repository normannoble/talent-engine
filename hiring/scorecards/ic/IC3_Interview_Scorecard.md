## Level Context: What IC3 Looks Like

IC3 is a **self-directed senior contributor** who leads project-level work and multiplies team effectiveness. Evaluate for:

- **Technical leadership** — Drives technical quality and standards
- **Mentorship** — Makes others more effective
- **Project ownership** — Delivers complex work from ambiguous requirements
- **Influence** — Shapes how the team works, not just what they ship

**Differentiation from IC2:** IC3 improves the team, not just their own output.
**Differentiation from IC4:** IC3 impacts one team; IC4 impacts multiple teams or the organization.
