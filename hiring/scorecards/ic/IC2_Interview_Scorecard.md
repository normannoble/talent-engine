## Level Context: What IC2 Looks Like

IC2 is a **solid professional contributor** who delivers independently on well-scoped work. Evaluate for:

- **Independent execution** — Can complete features with minimal oversight
- **Reliable delivery** — Estimates reasonably and delivers on commitments
- **Growing expertise** — Solid foundation, actively developing deeper skills
- **Professional habits** — Code quality, testing, documentation

**Differentiation from IC1:** IC2 works independently; IC1 needs significant guidance.
**Differentiation from IC3:** IC2 executes well; IC3 also mentors others and drives technical improvements.

**What to assess:** Programming proficiency, code quality, understanding of fundamentals.
