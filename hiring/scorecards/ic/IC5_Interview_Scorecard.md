## Level Context: What IC5 Looks Like

IC5 is the **highest individual contributor level**, with company-wide and industry-level impact. Evaluate for:

- **Company-level impact** — Shapes technical vision and strategy for the organization
- **Industry influence** — Recognized thought leader with external presence
- **Transformative contributions** — Solves previously unsolved problems
- **Building the next generation** — Develops Staff engineers into future technical leaders

**Differentiation from IC4:** IC4 has organizational impact; IC5 shapes company direction and influences industry.

**Note:** IC5 is an extremely senior hire. Candidates should have demonstrated extraordinary impact over sustained periods. This level is rare.

## Competency Assessment

### 1. Technical Vision & Strategy

**What to assess:** Company-level technical vision, strategic thinking, anticipating the future.

**IC5 Expectations:**
- Articulates technical vision that shapes company direction
- Anticipates technology trends and positions the organization accordingly
- Makes decisions with 3-5+ year time horizons
- Creates technical strategy that becomes competitive advantage

**Sample Questions:**
- Tell me about a technical vision you shaped at the company level. What was its impact?
- How do you think about where technology is heading in your domain?
- Describe a strategic technical bet you made. How did it play out?
- How do you balance near-term delivery with long-term technical positioning?
