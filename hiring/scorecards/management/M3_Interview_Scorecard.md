## Level Context: What M3 Looks Like

M3 is a **senior engineering leader** who manages managers and shapes organizational capability. Evaluate for:

- **Developing managers** — Grows M1s and M2s into effective leaders
- **Organizational leadership** — Shapes how engineering operates at scale
- **Strategic contribution** — Contributes directly to company direction
- **Technical credibility** — Maintains credibility with Staff+ engineers

**Differentiation from M2:** M2 manages ICs; M3 manages managers and shapes culture.
**Differentiation from E1:** M3 leads engineering capability; E1 owns domain-level business outcomes.

**Note:** M3 is a senior hire. Look for demonstrated success developing other managers and building organizational capability.

### 1. Developing Managers

**What to assess:** Growing management capability, coaching, delegation with development.

**M3 Expectations:**
- Develops M1s and M2s into more effective managers
- Delegates in ways that build capability, not just distribute work
- Creates management culture with shared practices and standards
- Builds bench strength and succession planning

**Sample Questions:**
- Tell me about managers you've developed. Where are they now?
- How do you coach a manager who is struggling?
- Describe your approach to delegation. How do you balance oversight with development?
- How do you build consistent management practices across your organization?
