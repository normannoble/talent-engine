## Level Context: What M2 Looks Like

M2 is a **proficient manager** who operates independently with minimal oversight. Evaluate for:

- **Independent operation** — Handles complex situations without escalation
- **High-performing teams** — Builds and maintains excellent team health and output
- **Sound judgment** — Makes good people decisions confidently
- **Systems thinking** — Creates processes that help the team scale

**Differentiation from M1:** M1 learns with guidance; M2 operates autonomously.
**Differentiation from M3:** M2 manages ICs; M3 manages managers.

**Note:** M2 is an experienced manager role. Look for a track record of independent, effective management.

## Competency Assessment

### 1. Independent People Management

**What to assess:** Handling complex situations, sound judgment, confident decision-making.

**M2 Expectations:**
- Handles complex people situations independently (performance issues, conflicts, retention)
- Makes sound hiring, promotion, and termination decisions
- Builds trust and operates without close oversight from own manager
- Demonstrates mature judgment in ambiguous situations

**Sample Questions:**
- Tell me about a complex performance situation you handled. Walk me through your approach.
- Describe a difficult termination decision you made. How did you handle it?
- How do you handle a situation where two team members are in conflict?
- Tell me about a time you disagreed with your manager on a people decision. What happened?
