## Level Context: What M1 Looks Like

M1 is a **first-level manager** learning management fundamentals while maintaining technical credibility. Evaluate for:

- **People leadership potential** — Natural ability to lead and develop others
- **Learning orientation** — Openness to growth, feedback, and making mistakes
- **Technical credibility** — Can guide and support team technically
- **Judgment development** — Reasonable instincts about people situations

**Differentiation from IC3:** M1 leads people; IC3 leads through technical influence.
**Differentiation from M2:** M1 is learning with guidance; M2 operates independently.

**Note:** M1 is often a first management role. Evaluate potential and instincts, not just track record.

## Competency Assessment

### 1. People Leadership Potential

**What to assess:** Natural leadership ability, interest in developing others, team-first mindset.

**M1 Expectations:**
- Demonstrates genuine interest in leading and developing people
- Shows empathy and ability to understand others' perspectives
- Has reasonable instincts about people situations
- Takes responsibility for team outcomes, not just individual contribution

**Sample Questions:**
- Why do you want to move into management?
- Tell me about a time you helped someone on your team succeed.
- How would you handle a team member who is underperforming?
- Describe a situation where you put the team's needs ahead of your own.
