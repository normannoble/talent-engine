# 30-60-90 Day Onboarding: Senior Director of Engineering (E2)

## Overview

**Level:** E2 — Senior Director of Engineering
**Focus:** Assessing cross-org effectiveness, CTO partnership, executive leadership
**Manager's Role:** CTO partnership, strategic alignment, organizational authority

E2s operate across multiple domains and partner closely with the CTO. The goal is to quickly assess organizational effectiveness and begin shaping engineering at scale.

---

## Days 1-30: Learn

### Theme: Organizational & Executive Assessment

**Primary Objectives:**
- Assess engineering organization comprehensively
- Build close partnership with CTO
- Understand company strategy and board context
- Map cross-functional executive relationships

### Week 1: Senior Executive Immersion

| Day | Focus | Activities |
|-----|-------|------------|
| 1 | Welcome | CEO intro, company strategy, board context |
| 2 | CTO Partnership | Deep alignment with CTO on priorities and partnership |
| 3 | Organization | Engineering org overview—domains, leaders, health |
| 4 | Business | Company financials, metrics, competitive landscape |
| 5 | Executives | Executive team meetings, cross-functional leadership |

**Key Meetings to Schedule:**
- [ ] CTO 1:1 (multiple times per week initially)
- [ ] CEO introduction and context
- [ ] Extended 1:1s with E1s/Directors
- [ ] Executive team members
- [ ] Board members (if appropriate)
- [ ] Key customers and partners (if appropriate)
- [ ] Product and business leadership

### Weeks 2-4: Comprehensive Assessment

**Learning Goals:**
- [ ] Deep understanding of engineering organizational health
- [ ] Assessment of E1/Director team capability
- [ ] View of cross-domain coordination and effectiveness
- [ ] Understanding of technology strategy execution
- [ ] Map of company strategic priorities and how engineering enables them

**Activities:**
- E1/Director assessment conversations
- Skip-level meetings across engineering
- Cross-domain coordination assessment
- Technology strategy review
- Executive team integration
- Document organizational assessment

**Assessment Milestones:**
- [ ] Engineering organizational health understood
- [ ] E1 team assessed
- [ ] Cross-domain effectiveness mapped
- [ ] CTO partnership established
- [ ] Executive integration underway

### 30-Day Check-in

**CTO Discussion Points:**
- What's your assessment of the engineering organization?
- How is the E1/Director team?
- What are the biggest cross-org challenges?
- How can we partner most effectively?
- What's your 60-day plan?

**Success Indicators:**
- [ ] Comprehensive organizational assessment complete
- [ ] CTO partnership operating
- [ ] E1 relationships established
- [ ] Executive integration progressing
- [ ] Strategic priorities clear

## Key Relationships to Build

| Who | Why | When to Meet |
|-----|-----|--------------|
| CTO | Primary partnership | Multiple times weekly |
| E1s (direct reports) | Leadership, development | Weekly each |
| CEO | Company strategy, visibility | First month, regular |
| Executive team | Cross-functional partnership | Weekly with key partners |
| Board | Governance, credibility | As appropriate |
| Industry peers | External perspective | Ongoing |

## Organizational Assessment Framework

### Engineering Organization Health
- Cross-domain coordination
- Delivery effectiveness
- Technical strategy execution
- Talent and culture
- Innovation capacity

### Leadership Team Assessment
- E1 capability and potential
- Succession pipeline
- Leadership culture
- Decision-making effectiveness
- Collaboration patterns

### Strategic Alignment
- Company strategy enablement
- Technology investment effectiveness
- Competitive positioning
- Risk management
- External reputation

---

## CTO Partnership Model

### What Makes It Work
- Clear division of responsibilities
- Regular, frequent communication
- Mutual trust and respect
- Complementary strengths
- Shared vision for engineering

### Key Questions to Align On
- How do we divide domains/responsibilities?
- How do we make decisions together?
- How do we communicate externally?
- How do we handle disagreements?
- What does success look like for our partnership?

---

## Common E2 Onboarding Challenges

| Challenge | Solution |
|-----------|----------|
| Unclear CTO partnership model | Discuss explicitly; define clearly; iterate |
| Weak E1s in organization | Assess honestly; develop or transition; build bench |
| Cross-domain dysfunction | Invest in coordination; address structural issues |
| Board expectations unclear | Clarify with CTO; understand governance rhythm |
| External presence undeveloped | Build intentionally; align with company needs |
