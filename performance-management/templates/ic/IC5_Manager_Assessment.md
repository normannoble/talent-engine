## Instructions

This assessment synthesizes the employee's self-assessment, peer feedback, and your own observations to provide a comprehensive evaluation of performance against IC5 (Distinguished Software Engineer) expectations.

**Guidelines:**
- **Evaluate against IC5 level** - Use the job description Success Outcomes as your rubric
- **Use specific evidence** - Reference concrete company-level impacts, transformative contributions, and industry influence
- **Synthesize all inputs** - Consider self, peer, and your own observations
- **Be balanced and fair** - Acknowledge strengths while being clear about gaps
- **Explain your ratings** - Provide reasoning for assessments, especially divergence from self/peer input
- **Focus on patterns** - Assess sustained company-level and industry impact
- **This will be calibrated** - Your assessments will be reviewed with senior leadership before finalization

**Rating Scale:**
- **Exceptional** - Rare, outstanding performance well beyond IC5 level; truly exceptional and unexpected
- **Exceeding Expectation** - Consistently performing above already-high IC5 expectations
- **Meeting Expectation** - Consistently achieving success outcomes defined for IC5
- **Below Expectation** - Performance falling short of IC5 expectations in meaningful ways
- **Unsatisfactory** - Performance significantly below expectations; core outcomes not achieved
