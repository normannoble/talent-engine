## Instructions

You've been asked to provide peer feedback for [Employee Name] as part of their performance review. Your input helps provide perspective on day-to-day collaboration, technical contributions, and how they show up as a teammate.

**Guidelines:**
- **Be specific** - Provide concrete examples where possible
- **Be honest and constructive** - Vague praise isn't helpful; neither is unconstructive criticism
- **Focus on work and behaviors** - Comment on what they do and how they work, not personality traits
- **Only comment where you have direct observation** - It's okay to say "I don't have enough visibility into this area"
- **Your feedback is confidential** - Your manager will receive this but will summarize themes without attributing specific comments to you

**Context for IC3 (Senior Software Engineer) expectations:**
- Self-directed technical contributor who leads project-level work
- Multiplies team effectiveness through expertise, mentorship, and quality leadership
- Deep technical expertise with sound judgment in ambiguous situations
- Owns significant technical areas and influences team practices
