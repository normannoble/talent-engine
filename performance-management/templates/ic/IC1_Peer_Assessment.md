## Instructions

You've been asked to provide peer feedback for [Employee Name] as part of their performance review. Your input helps provide perspective on day-to-day collaboration, learning approach, and how they show up as a teammate.

**Guidelines:**
- **Be specific** - Provide concrete examples where possible
- **Be honest and constructive** - Vague praise isn't helpful; neither is unconstructive criticism
- **Focus on work and behaviors** - Comment on what they do and how they work, not personality traits

- **Your feedback is confidential** - Your manager will receive this but will summarize themes without attributing specific comments to you

**Context for IC1 (Associate Software Engineer) expectations:**

- Building foundation through guidance and practice
- Expected to ask questions, incorporate feedback, and show consistent improvement
- Success is defined by growth, not perfection
