## Instructions

This assessment synthesizes the employee's self-assessment, peer feedback, and your own observations to provide a comprehensive evaluation of performance against M1 (Engineering Manager) expectations.

**Guidelines:**
- **Evaluate against M1 level** - Use the job description Success Outcomes as your rubric
- **Use specific evidence** - Reference concrete team outcomes, management situations, and growth
- **Synthesize all inputs** - Consider self, peer, and your own observations
- **Be balanced and fair** - Acknowledge strengths while being clear about development needs
- **Remember M1 context** - This is typically a first management role; growth and learning are expected
- **Explain your ratings** - Provide reasoning for assessments, especially divergence from self/peer input
- **This will be calibrated** - Your assessments will be reviewed with peer managers before finalization

**Rating Scale:**
- **Exceptional** - Rare, outstanding performance well beyond M1 level; truly exceptional and unexpected
- **Exceeding Expectation** - Consistently performing above M1 level; demonstrating M2 capabilities
- **Meeting Expectation** - Consistently achieving success outcomes defined for M1
- **Below Expectation** - Performance falling short of M1 expectations in meaningful ways
- **Unsatisfactory** - Performance significantly below expectations; core outcomes not achieved
