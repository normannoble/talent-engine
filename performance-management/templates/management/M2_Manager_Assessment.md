## Instructions

This assessment synthesizes the employee's self-assessment, peer feedback, and your own observations to provide a comprehensive evaluation of performance against M2 (Senior Engineering Manager) expectations.

**Guidelines:**
- **Evaluate against M2 level** - Use the job description Success Outcomes as your rubric
- **Use specific evidence** - Reference concrete team outcomes, management decisions, and systems built
- **Synthesize all inputs** - Consider self, peer, and your own observations
- **Be balanced and fair** - Acknowledge strengths while being clear about gaps
- **Explain your ratings** - Provide reasoning, especially for divergence from self/peer input
- **Focus on patterns** - Assess sustained independent management capability
- **This will be calibrated** - Your assessments will be reviewed with peer managers before finalization

**Rating Scale:**
- **Exceptional** - Rare, outstanding performance well beyond M2 level; truly exceptional and unexpected
- **Exceeding Expectation** - Consistently performing above M2 level; demonstrating M3 capabilities
- **Meeting Expectation** - Consistently achieving success outcomes defined for M2
- **Below Expectation** - Performance falling short of M2 expectations in meaningful ways
- **Unsatisfactory** - Performance significantly below expectations; core outcomes not achieved
